# Nutrition Strategy

This note is the single source of truth for nutrition targets. Agents and reviews read targets from here and only from here — update this note and everything downstream follows.

## Daily Targets

- Calories: 2300 kcal
- Protein: 160 g
- Carbs: 230 g
- Fat: 75 g

## Approach

- Mild deficit (~300 kcal under estimated maintenance) aimed at the ~0.25 kg/week loss in [[Goals]]; same targets every day — lifting-day cycling is complexity this phase does not need.
- Protein anchor at every meal; the rest of the plate is negotiable.
- Full Intake tables in the daily note only when the trend stalls; right now the regime is daily weigh-ins plus honest logging of anything unusual.

## Prep Cycles

- Sunday batch-cook: one pot of grains, two proteins (usually chicken thighs + lentils); weekday lunches are assembled, not cooked.

## Foods to Emphasize

- Skyr/yogurt, eggs, chicken thighs, lentils, oats, frozen vegetables, fruit for the sweet tooth.

## Foods to Limit / Avoid

- Alcohol outside weekends; sugary drinks entirely; no snacking after 22:00 — the late-night desk graze is the single biggest historical leak.

## Eating Out Rules

- Pick the protein anchor first, then choose the venue's version of it; estimate and log anyway (estimates beat blanks); one no-questions-asked meal per week.
