# Goals

## Primary Goals

- Train three times a week (two lifting sessions, one easy conditioning) every week through winter — consistency is the goal, not load.
- Bring bodyweight from ~79 kg down to ~76 kg at roughly 0.25 kg per week, without losing the bar lifts.

## Secondary Goals

- Daily band pull-aparts to undo desk shoulders; attached to the morning coffee, not to willpower.
- Median sleep at 7 hours; tracked loosely via the journal, not instrumented.

## Current Priorities

- Show up and log it. Sessions capped at 75 minutes so they cannot crowd out deep work.

## Why These Goals Matter

- Deep-work energy visibly tracks training regularity, and deadline seasons kill any plan that relies on motivation — so the plan has to be boring enough to survive a paper deadline.

## Constraints

- Home setup only: barbell, plates up to 120 kg, adjustable dumbbells, bands. No machines, no spotter — so no grinding maximal singles.
- Right wrist flares up on heavy pressing; cuffs on for bench, and volume before intensity.

## Current Approach

- Upper/Lower split, A/B alternation, logged per session in Training/Logs (the [[Fitness/Training/Training-Log.base|Training Log]] view is the audit trail).
- Nutrition per [[Strategy|Nutrition Strategy]] — targets live there and only there.

## Success Markers

- Weekly bodyweight average trending down ~0.25 kg/week in the [[Fitness/Tracking/Bodyweight.base|Bodyweight]] view.
- Every planned session actually has a log note; a missing log is a missed session, no exceptions.

## Non-Goals

- No meets, no bulking before spring, no supplement experiments, no new programs before March — program-hopping is the failure mode being designed against.
