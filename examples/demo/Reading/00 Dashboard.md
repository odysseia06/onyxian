# Reading Dashboard

The pipeline: capture fast into Inbox, decide later — keep into Articles, distill what deserves forever into Evergreen, and link useful notes into the domains they serve instead of duplicating them. The [[Reading-Pipeline.base|Reading Pipeline]] views show every stage.

## Inbox Discipline

- Capture with the Quick Capture template; one idea per note; a `url` and a one-line "why" beat a wall of paste.
- Triage on your own rhythm. Keep (Articles), distill (Evergreen), or let it die in the inbox — dying there is a feature.
